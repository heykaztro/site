Origin Story

From Kerala
To The World

Kaztro — Muhammed Ramees MP — is one of Kerala's most-followed gaming and lifestyle creators. Starting with BGMI gameplay, he built a 2.4M+ community across three YouTube channels and Instagram without leaving his home state.

Now part of S8UL, India's elite esports collective, Kaztro creates content that spans competitive gaming, real-talk vlogs, and a dedicated kids channel. His audience isn't just watching — they're grinding alongside him.

With a fast-growing UAE/GCC fanbase, he's actively building for the next chapter: a localized content hub targeting Middle East audiences across gaming, tech, automotive, and lifestyle verticals.
